## Agile scoping for quick wins with AI
Description:
Define a process to scope AI projects for maximum early impact and fast learning. Focus on identifying business problems that are tractable with available data/resources and can yield measurable value using existing AI/ML techniques.

Checklist:
- [ ] Identify 3-5 candidate business problems suitable for quick AI wins.
- [ ] Evaluate available data and AI maturity.
- [ ] Draft one-pager for each, scoping for timelines and delivery.
- [ ] Get feedback from stakeholders.
- [ ] Select 1-2 for rapid prototyping.

Labels: skill, ai, project, priority:high
